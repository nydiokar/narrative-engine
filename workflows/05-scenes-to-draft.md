# Workflow 05: Scenes to Draft

**Goal**: Assemble scenes into a complete narrative draft.

## Steps

1. **Scene Writer Finalizes Prose**
   - Polishes scene prose
   - Ensures consistent tone across scenes

2. **Continuity Editor Final Check**
   - Full-narrative hard gate verification
   - Causal soundness throughout
   - Character intentionality across all chapters
   - World rule consistency across entire draft
   - Discourse contract adherence

3. **Showrunner Assembles Draft**
   - Orders scenes in sujet order
   - Adds transitions where needed
   - Produces complete draft manuscript

## Output
- Complete narrative draft
- Ready for Workflow 06 (Editorial Passes)
