# Workflow 07: Critique and Revision

**Goal**: Evaluate the draft against quality criteria and iterate until passing.

## Steps

1. **Critic Assesses Draft**
   - Evaluates all five quality dimensions
   - Produces Critique Contract
   - Determines pass/fail

2. **If Pass**: Workflow complete. Archive draft.

3. **If Fail**:
   - Revision Agent parses Critique Contract
   - Identifies specific issues and affected contracts
   - Applies targeted revisions
   - Returns revised artifact for re-assessment
   - Loop continues until all dimensions pass

4. **Director Approves Final Version**
   - Signs off on final draft
   - Updates story status to "final"

## Output
- Final narrative draft
- Revision log (`stories/<story-name>/revision-log.md`)
- Critique Contract (archive)
