# Workflow 06: Scenes to Draft

**Goal**: Assemble scenes into a complete narrative draft.

## Steps

1. **Scene Writer Finalizes Prose**
   - Polishes scene prose
   - Ensures consistent tone across scenes

2. **Continuity Editor Final Check**
   - Full-narrative consistency verification
   - Temporal and spatial continuity
   - Modality state continuity across all scenes

3. **Director Assembles Draft**
   - Orders scenes in sujet order (may differ from fabula order)
   - Adds transitions where needed
   - Produces complete draft manuscript

## Output
- Complete narrative draft (`stories/<story-name>/draft.md`)
- Ready for Workflow 07
