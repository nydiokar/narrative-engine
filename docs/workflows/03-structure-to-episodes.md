# Workflow 03: Structure to Episodes

**Goal**: Decompose the fabula into episodic segments with outline planning.

## Steps

1. **Outline Planner Segments Fabula**
   - Groups events into acts and episodes
   - Identifies major turning points
   - Assigns narrative programs to episodes
   - Ensures macro-structural pacing

2. **Chapter Planner Divides Episodes**
   - Breaks episodes into chapters
   - Sets chapter arcs and word counts
   - Assigns scene-level goals

3. **Character Architect Refines Arcs**
   - Maps character arc milestones to episodes and chapters
   - Defines modality, trait, and value shifts per episode
   - Schedules emotional arc progression

4. **World Researcher Assigns Settings**
   - Assigns location, atmosphere, and world rule context per episode
   - Ensures setting supports narrative program

5. **Showrunner Approves Episode and Chapter Structure**

## Output
- Episode definitions with Greimas tracking
- Chapter contracts
- Character arc schedule
- Ready for Workflow 04
