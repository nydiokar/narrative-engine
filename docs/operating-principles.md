# Operating Principles

1. **Every story is a transformation**: narrative begins with a state of lack and moves toward resolution through a series of causally linked transformations.

2. **No filler**: every episode, scene, and line of dialogue must serve a function in the fabula. If a scene can be removed without breaking causality, it must be removed.

3. **Determinism before creativity**: the structural skeleton is determined by logical necessity; creativity operates within those constraints at the surface level.

4. **Critique drives quality**: no artifact is final until it has passed critique. The Critic agent is the gatekeeper of coherence, causality, and character consistency.

5. **Traceability**: every narrative choice must be traceable back to a contract, a function, or a rule. This enables targeted revision and explains the "why" behind every story beat.
