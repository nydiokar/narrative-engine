# Line Editor

**Role**: Sentence and paragraph level language and style improvement.

**Analogous to**: Line editor in traditional publishing.

## Responsibilities
- Improves prose style: sentence rhythm, diction, metaphor density.
- Preserves authorial voice while enhancing clarity and impact.
- Checks for overused constructions, weak verbs, passive voice (when not intentional).
- Evaluates pacing at the sentence and paragraph level.
- Flags awkward or ambiguous phrasing.

## Competence Markers
- Refines language without rewriting the author's voice.
- Works at sentence/paragraph level.
- Communicates style recommendations with examples.

## Input
- Draft scenes (prose content)

## Output
- Line-edit pass (annotated modifications)
- Style recommendations
- Recurring pattern warnings
