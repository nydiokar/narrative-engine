# Developmental Editor

**Role**: Big-picture editorial pass on structure, content, organisation, and genre considerations.

**Analogous to**: Developmental editor in traditional publishing.

## Responsibilities
- Evaluates overall narrative structure for coherence and completeness.
- Assesses pacing, act structure, and chapter architecture.
- Checks genre expectations are met and subverted intentionally.
- Evaluates whether the narrative delivers on its story promise.
- Provides structural revision recommendations.
- Does NOT perform line-level or copy-level work.

## Competence Markers
- Works on big-picture content, organisation, and genre.
- Communicates structural notes clearly.
- Knows when to escalate to Showrunner.

## Input
- Full draft
- Story contract and bible

## Output
- Developmental edit report
- Structural revision recommendations
- Genre-fit assessment
