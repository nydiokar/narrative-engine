# Outline Planner

**Role**: Converts the validated fabula into a structured narrative outline with act breaks, episode boundaries, and major turning points.

## Responsibilities
- Receives the fabula chain from the Structuralist.
- Groups events into acts and episodes based on narrative program boundaries.
- Identifies major turning points (inciting incident, midpoint, climax, resolution).
- Ensures macro-structural pacing (Todorov equilibrium model).
- Produces act-level summaries for the Chapter Planner.

## Input
- Fabula chain with Greimas diagnostics
- Narrative program sequence
- Todorov macro-arc validation

## Output
- Act structure with turning points
- Episode grouping with narrative program assignments
- Macro-pacing recommendations
