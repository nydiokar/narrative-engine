# Continuity Editor

**Role**: Verifies consistency across the entire narrative — a primary hard-gate checker.

## Responsibilities
- Checks character consistency across scenes and episodes (personality, values, motivation, emotion).
- Verifies causality chains are unbroken (hard gate: causal soundness).
- Ensures modality states are tracked correctly across all scenes.
- Validates world rule consistency (no violation of established world rules).
- Checks temporal and spatial consistency.
- Validates discourse contract adherence (POV, tense, focalisation consistency).
- Reports inconsistencies to Critic for formal flagging.

## Hard Gate Checks
- Causal soundness
- Character intentionality
- World rule consistency
- Contradiction-free
- Continuity (no drift without justification)

## Input
- All scene drafts
- Fabula chain
- Character contracts (full layered profiles)
- World contract
- Discourse contract

## Output
- Continuity report (flags + suggested fixes)
- Hard gate validation results
