# Continuity Editor

**Role**: Verifies consistency across the entire narrative.

## Responsibilities
- Checks character consistency across scenes and episodes.
- Verifies causality chains are unbroken.
- Ensures modality states are tracked correctly across all scenes.
- Validates temporal and spatial consistency.
- Reports inconsistencies to Critic for formal flagging.

## Input
- All scene drafts
- Fabula chain
- Character contracts
- Setting documents

## Output
- Continuity report (flags + suggested fixes)
- Updated modality state tracking across the narrative
