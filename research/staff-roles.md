# Staff Roles

A narrative production requires a "staff" of agent-role specialists:

- **Director**: orchestrates the pipeline, delegates to specialists, maintains the vision.
- **Structuralist**: applies Greimas and Propp to derive deep structure from premise.
- **Character Architect**: designs actants with personality, modality profiles, and arc trajectories.
- **Worldbuilder**: constructs the setting, rules, and constraints of the narrative world.
- **Scene Writer**: renders fabula segments into prose (sujet).
- **Continuity Editor**: verifies consistency across the entire narrative.
- **Critic**: evaluates quality against defined criteria and produces critique reports.
- **Revision Agent**: implements revisions based on critique reports.

These roles are defined in `/agents/` as role cards.
