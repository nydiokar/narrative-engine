# Genre Map

Genre constrains which actantial configurations, Proppian functions, and modality profiles are available.

| Genre | Typical Actantial Profile | Preferred Functions |
|-------|--------------------------|---------------------|
| Fantasy | Strong Helper/Opponent presence | Quest, Magic Agent, Transfiguration |
| Thriller | Subject as Detective, Hidden Opponent | Exposure, Pursuit, Rescue |
| Romance | Subject & Object as mutual Subjects | Recognition, Wedding |
| Tragedy | Subject's modality conflict (want vs must) | Lack, Punishment, Exposure |
| Comedy | Misaligned actantial perceptions | Unrecognized Arrival, Claim |

The Genre Map informs the Structuralist agent's initial function selection and actant assignment.
