# Quality Principles

The Critic agent evaluates narratives against these dimensions:

## Coherence
- Does every event follow causally from prior events?
- Are actant roles consistent throughout the fabula?

## Completeness
- Is every narrative program carried to its terminal state?
- Are all Proppian functions that were opened eventually closed?

## Character Consistency
- Do characters act in accordance with their established modality profiles?
- Are personality traits reflected in dialogue and action?

## No-Filler Compliance
- Does every scene serve a structural function?
- Could any scene be removed without breaking the fabula?

## Structural Fidelity
- Does the fabula conform to the Greimasian actantial model?
- Are Proppian functions applied in the correct sequence?
